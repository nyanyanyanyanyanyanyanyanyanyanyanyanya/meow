Pokémon Adventures, or Pokespe (ポケスペ / ポケSP), is a Japanese manga written by Hidenori Kusaka and currently illustrated by Satoshi Yamamoto. X&Y is the twelfth chapter in the manga, and is based on the XY games. While many of the characters and situations resemble their game iterations, they often don't 1:1 recreate the games, so are self-contained to their own continuity. The X&Y chapters are after the Emerald chapter, around the time of ORAS. Professor Sycamore, as a prominent character in XY, makes a sizable appearance in the manga, in which his personality and appearance are faithfully recreated. While his actions and general storyline differ from his game counterpart, I still feel like manga Professor Sycamore is an enjoyable and respectable portrayal of him!

Chapter Summaries

For super Sycamore fans who want to focus on his manga appearances, here is a list of every chapter in which Professor Sycamore makes a notable appearance, organized from earliest to latest. Please keep in mind that I'm only covering Professor Sycamore's appearances in the manga, so no extra story aspects will be covered unless they directly pertain to him.

X&Y001: An X-cuse to Come Out and Play (ガルーラ、待つ, Kangaskhan, Wait?)
Professor Sycamore makes a minor appearance, and his first appearance, in this chapter in the form of a call to Trevor, who is running an errand on his behalf. It's short but cute establishing Trevor doing things for the professor! The delivery in question is actually a shipment of Kalos starters, including Fennekin, Chespin, and Froakie. Trevor is there to deliver the starts to X(Calem in the games) because X has become a shut in. However, things in Vanivelle take a turn nobody could expect...
X&Y003: Inn-teresting Developments (ホルード、奪う, Diggersby, Seize?)
The next time we see Professor Sycamore is when Trevor brings him up when speaking to the other Kalos friends. They're at an inn in Aquacorde Town after a terrible incident happened in Vanivelle, but before they could get into any details, they're being ambushed by the nefarious Team Flare.
X&Y004: They Have a Flare for a Li'l Kanga-Napping (ハリマロン、刺す, Chespin, Pierce?)
This chapter is the first time we see the professor outside of calls or references. He receives a panicked recording from Trevor who was there at the time of the Vanivelle incident, and he discusses the importance of the recorded legendary Pokémon and figuring out the truth of what happened in Vanivelle Town, and how important it was at the time that the boy was able to record the incident. The recording was actually taken with a Holocaster, which is the invention of Mr.Lysandre - the same Mr.Lysandre that Professor Sycamore is talking to in this chapter! It ends on a cliffhanger...
X&Y006: Kangaskhan… Kangascan't (ガルーラ、試す, Kangaskhan, Attempt?)
This chapter continues right where the previous chapter leaves off, with Professor Sycamore and Mr.Lysandre talking about the recorded footage, and coming to the conclusion that those indeed are the legendary Pokemon of myth based on the provided footage. It's also revealed that Lysandre and Professor Sycamore are friends and/or colleagues, with Lysandre supporting the lab with his funding in exchange for Professsor Sycamore providing information on his research. Hmm... well, the two seem to be good friends!
X&Y010: What Does Charmander Do When It Dozes? (ヒトカゲ、まどろむ, Charmander, Snooze?)
This chapter is when Professor Sycamore finally meets the Kalos friend group in person. He'd only known Trevor from online correspondence, so he's happy to finally meet everyone, and that they are safe and secure! However, after everything the friends have been through at this point, X doesn't trust Professor Sycamore at all. Professor Syamore attempts to bridge the communication gap via a Pokémon battle while he sends the Kalos friends to get their Holocaster devices repaired by Lysandre at his cafe.
X&Y011: Chespin Sows the Seeds of Change (ハリマロン、起つ, Chespin, Rise?)
X, whose heart wasn't very much in the battle, treats the affair flippantly and lets Chespin get battered. Professor Sycamore offers an opportunity for X to throw the battle but Chespin shows an incredible amount of fortitude when he won't back down. This inspires X to finally start issuing commands even if he didn't quite care for Professor Sycamore's methodology. After the battle's end, X also requests to take Charmander, but provides no explanation as to why. The two leave the lab together, sensing danger!
X&Y014: Pangoro Poses a Problem (ゴロンダ、探る, Pangoro, Search?)
Professor Sycamore has an interview planned at prism tower with Alexa, who is a journalist in Lumiose. However, when Alexa arrives at the interview, she finds that it's already taken place with her editor-in-chief, her boss. She's very confused, but the editor requests Professor Sycamore leave, after all, he already did his interview and they wouldn't want to waste his valuable time...
X&Y015: Charging After Electrike (ラクライ、追う, Electrike, Pursue?)
This chapter involves what Professor Sycamore and X spoke about while they left thelab together, which involves Professor Sycamore explaining the myths surrounding the Kalos legendaries, Xerneas and Yveltal. Trevor wants to meet up with Professor Sycamore but the friend group disagrees, they don't believe they can trust him, especially because Professor Sycamore dismissed their concerns about Lysandre being suspicious. They decide to avoid him...
X&Y016: Corphish Pinches (ヘイガニ、はさむ, Corphish, Nip?)
Professor Sycamore makes a small appearance in this chapter as a flashback when X recalls him: Sycamore is, after all, the one who gave him the Pokédex. He still doesn'tlike him but he can't deny how useful it is to have his help...
X&Y035: Scizor Defends (ハッサム、受ける, Scizor, Defend?)
As you can see, there's suddenly a big jump between Professor Sycamore appearances in the manga! He doesn't come back to play until several chapters later! Lysandre mentions that Professor Sycamore has found out the truth in regards to Team Flare and is making moves against them. Trevor receives a message from an extremely repentent Professor Sycamore, who apologizes to the Kalos friends for not appropriately hearing them out about their suspicions in regards to Lysandre. After Trevor sent him footage of what happened in the Team Flare HQ, Sycamore was hurt and betrayed that his friend Lysandre would be the leader of Team Flare! He wants to make up for his misstep, so he is going to Anistar City...hearing his desperation, the friends decide to agree to meet him there!
X&Y036: Hawlucha Attack (ルチャブル、襲う, Hawlucha, Attack?)
This chapter is crazy! The Kalos friendgroup and their tagalongs arrive in Anistar City to meet up with Sycamore, who is researching the crystal sundial in the cicty.But by the time the friends get there, Professor Sycamore is hanging off the sundial percariously, being attacked by a swarm of Hawlucha! He even falls! But he's okay, because Froakie's frubbles cushioned his landing. After the Hawlucha attack is waylayed, Sycamore is thankful aand apologetic, and he tells them more about the sundial, its connection to mega evolution, and the ultimate weapon.
