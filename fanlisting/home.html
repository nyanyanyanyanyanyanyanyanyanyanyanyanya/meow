Welcome to the Professor Sycamore Fanlisting!

This is a fanlisting website dedicated to Professor Sycamore from Pokémon XY. Are you a fan of Kalos' brilliant and charming Pokémon professor? Then you're in the right spot!

About Professor Sycamore

Professor Sycamore is the Pokémon researcher in the Kalos region, known for his studies on Mega Evolution, his approachable personality, and is charming-but-scatterbrained demeanor. He is the player character's guide during the course of XY. For more information on Professor Sycamore, please consider visiting South Boulevard, a fanshrine for him. Or, better yet, play the games!

Why We Love Him

From his stylish swoopy hair, his passion for Pokémon, or his optimistic outlook, Professor Sycamore is an inspiration to so many people, both in the context of the game and in our world, too. He represents a lot of things to a lot of people. Plus, he's also super cute!
